# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Homeowners and business owners looking for clean energy solutions. They are environmentally conscious but also value performance, reliability, and modern technology.

## Product Purpose
TTT Solar provides cutting-edge solar turbine technology. The website aims to showcase these solutions, provide a solar calculator for potential customers, and build trust through a premium, future-forward brand presence.

## Brand Personality
Clean, Bold, Future-forward, Reliable.
3-word personality: Sustainable, Advanced, Premium.
Emotional goals: Confidence, Hope, Sophistication.

## Anti-references
- Generic "Corporate Green" SaaS templates.
- Cluttered, information-dense layouts without hierarchy.
- Low-fidelity, mobile-unfriendly interfaces.
- Standard "Stock Photo" look (prefer stylized, high-quality renders or icons).

## Design Principles
1. **Mobile-First & Thumb-Zone Focused**: Every layout must work flawlessly on mobile, with primary actions within reach of the thumb (Thumb Zone).
2. **Strict Spacing Rhythm**: Use only 4, 8, 12, 16, and 24px spacing to maintain a perfect visual rhythm and professional feel.
3. **High-Contrast Dark Elegance**: Maintain the deep dark background (#0a0a0a) with vibrant yellow (#eab308) accents and white typography for maximum readability and a premium "Pro" feel.
4. **Purposeful Micro-Interactions**: Use Framer Motion for subtle, organic transitions that provide feedback and delight without distracting from the content.
5. **No Slop Design**: Avoid generic AI-generated looking components. Every element should feel deliberate and handcrafted.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Targeting WCAG AA compliance. High contrast ratios for text on dark backgrounds. Large tap targets (>= 44px). Support for reduced motion.
